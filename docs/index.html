Overview

Civkit

The Civ Kit combines Nostr, Lightning and PGP to leverage anonymous communication, decentralized identity and instant settlement of trades between any 2 parties. It is completely experimental and use at your own risk.

Civkit Node

The CivKit Node is split in 3 components. API, Frontend and Chat. Each work together but each can work without the other. This was designed for users to bring their own chat tools and design their own frontends if needed. Build your own, and use our parts.

Staking Credentials

You only need an NPUB and a lightning wallet to trade. You register pay purchasing a staking credential. This low cost solution prevents cheaters and spam. Your identity is your npub so protect it wisely. You are the master of your reputation in this world.
